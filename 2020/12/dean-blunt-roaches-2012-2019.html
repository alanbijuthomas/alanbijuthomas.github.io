Dean Blunt - Roaches 2012-2019

World Music Group, 2020, hypnagogic pop/experimental

5th December 2020


Avant-garde prankster Dean Blunt has released some of the most forward-thinking music of the past decade and I'd even go so far as to say he's the greatest British artist of the 21st century. Roaches 2012-2019 compiles loosies, demos and one-offs that may have briefly surfaced on YouTube. The original release came out in March but I recently purchased the 2-hour $​up£r D€lux£ edition on Bandcamp Friday. The 53 tracks showcase Dean's vast and varied experiments, ranging from sultry dream pop to demented, dubby post-punk to abstract pastiches that defy the very notion of genre. There's that air of irony and plenty of lo-fi sampling that are perhaps the only constant features of Dean's work. Overall, it makes for a hazy, cryptic listen with tracks like 'NEVA' and 'NITRO GIRLS 3' always bringing me back. This compilation is the perfect introduction to the world of Dean Blunt.
