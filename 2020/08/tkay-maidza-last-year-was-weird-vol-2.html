Tkay Maidza - Last Year Was Weird, Vol. 2

4AD Records, 2020, pop rap

30th August 2020


Tkay Maidza is an Australian rapper who I'd heard of but never listened to. On her debut for 4AD Records, she comes through with what's probably my favourite hip hop release of 2020 so far. Just like its album cover, Last Year Was Weird, Vol. 2 is futuristic, diverse and full of swagger. Tkay hops genres with ease from the neo-soul of opener 'My Flowers' to the hip house of '24k' to the more industrial trap of JPEGMAFIA-assisted banger 'Awake'. Although it's all over the place stylistically, there's no misses on this concise EP as it's tied together by Tkay's catchy hooks, clever lyrics and some of the best pop rap production since BROCKHAMPTON's SATURATION trilogy.
