LOOΠΔ / ODD EYE CIRCLE - Mix & Match

BlockBerryCreative, 2017, k-pop

10th August 2020


While I had heard the odd K-pop song here and there, I'd not dived too deep into the scene. I ended up checking out YUKIKA's SOUL LADY due to the city pop influence and went down a rabbit hole, listening to a lot of K-pop. LOOΠΔ / ODD EYE CIRCLE is a sub-unit of LOOΠΔ and their debut EP Mix & Match is easily the best K-pop release I've heard thus far. Although I have absolutely no clue what the girls are singing about for the most part, the uplifting electropop sound is just pure ear candy. There's even some future bass sprinkled in too. Every track is addictive but the highlight is 'Girl Front'. It is pop perfection with gorgeous vocal melodies and one of the catchiest choruses I've ever heard. I may have to start stanning LOOΠΔ...
