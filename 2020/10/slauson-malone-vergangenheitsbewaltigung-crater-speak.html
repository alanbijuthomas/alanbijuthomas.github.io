Slauson Malone - Vergangenheitsbewältigung (Crater Speak)

Grand Closing, 2020, neo-soul/sound collage

9th October 2020


"Vergangenheitsbewältigung" is a German word that describes the process of coming to terms with the past and that's exactly what Slauson Malone does on the follow-up to 2019's sound collage, A Quiet Farwell, 2016–2018. This EP isn't as reliant on samples, instead exploring Malone's abilities as a singer/songwriter. Pairing his depressive tales with angelic guitar work and the occasional glitch, Slauson Malone has made a very different project, but one which is just as layered, intimate and emotive. I'm really not quite sure how to describe this project but tracks like 'Smile #6', 'THE MESSAGE 3: Blood' and 'The Wake Pt. 3 & 2' are achingly beautiful. With singles from Standing on the Corner, the new MIKE album and plenty more in 2020, the sLUms collective continue to make some of the most exciting music today.
