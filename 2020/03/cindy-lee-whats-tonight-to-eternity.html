Cindy Lee - What's Tonight to Eternity

W.25th, 2020, hypnagogic pop

14th March 2020


What's Tonight to Eternity is a record all about its surreal atmosphere. Just like he did as a member of indie rock band Women, Patrick Flegel takes and twists elements of pop music to the extreme. Borrowing from brill building as much as no wave, Patrick uses androgynous vocals, haunted melodies and disturbing distortion to craft bizarre textures. It is noisy yet dreamy, mysterious yet beautiful. Dedicated to former bandmate Chris Reimer, 'Heavy Metal' is perhaps the album's finest moment with its distant vocals, hypnotising riff and euphoric use of guitar feedback. The album works best as a singular experience and would probably serve well as a soundtrack to a dissociation. What's Tonight to Eternity is the best release I've heard so far this year and I plan to lose myself in its cathartic noise many a time.
