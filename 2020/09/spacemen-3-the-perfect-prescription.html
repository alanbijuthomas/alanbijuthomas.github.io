Spacemen 3 - The Perfect Prescription

Glass Records, 1987, neo-psychedelia/space rock

13th September 2020


Spacemen 3 were one of the greatest bands of the 80s, redefining what psychedelic music could be with their minimalist explorations. The Perfect Prescription is perhaps their best record as they successfully emulate a drug experience. From the initial dose on noisy 'Take Me to the Other Side' to the revelatory interactions with God on 'Walkin' With Jesus' to the bluesy, gospel-inspired 'Come Down Easy', Spacemen 3 manage to capture the highs and lows of a trip. My favourite track is the trip's peak, 'Ecstasy Symphony / Transparent Radiation (Flashback)' as J. Spaceman and Sonic Boom transform a Red Krayola freak-out into a serene, soothing piece. Spacemen 3's swirling guitar drones remain some of the trippiest music ever recorded. It's perfect for forgetting your troubles and floating along to.
