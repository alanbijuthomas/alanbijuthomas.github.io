DJ Screw - All Screwed Up, Vol. II

Bigtyme Recordz, 1995, chopped and screwed/southern hip hop

18th January 2020


DJ Screw was one of southern hip hop's many innovators, slowing down and spicing up tracks with scratches, delays and skips to create the genre of chopped and screwed. Lean was to the Houston scene what acid was to the hippies and DJ Screw's unique style attempted to replicate and enhance the wavy feels experienced on purple drank. All Screwed Up, Vol. II is widely considered his best mix and it's perfect for nocturnal listening with a series of hypnotic, haunting bangers that reflect on the paranoia of the gangsta rap lifestyle. 'My Mind Went Blank' is up there with Geto Boys' 'Mind Playing Tricks On Me' as one of hip hop's greatest explorations of mental health. Aside from DJ Screw's obvious influence on trap, cloud rap, vaporwave and even witch house, his style remains popular with practically every hit song getting its own "slowed and reverb" version uploaded to YouTube.
