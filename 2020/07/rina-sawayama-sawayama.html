Rina Sawayama - SAWAYAMA

Dirty Hit, 2020, dance-pop/contemporary r&b

19th July 2020


2017's RINA EP was one of the best pop projects of the last decade and left me excited for Rina Sawayama's debut album. SAWAYAMA delivers on all fronts and establishes Rina as one of modern pop's most exciting figures. I'm in love with the album's eclectic sonic palette, which is mostly rooted in 2000s dance-pop but also incorporates alternative rock guitars throughout. Opener 'Dynasty' is a powerful anthem which lays out the album's theme of the struggles faced by second-generation immigrants, something I can relate to. 'XS' is arguably the record's crown jewel with its catchy hook and powerful assault on capitalism ringing true, especially now. Rina even dabbles with metal à la Poppy on 'STFU!' and disco à la Dua Lipa on 'Comme Des Garçons'. Despite being a little front-loaded, SAWAYAMA is one of 2020's best in pop, tied together by Rina's great songwriting, top-notch production from Clarence Clarity and a consistent, engaging theme.
