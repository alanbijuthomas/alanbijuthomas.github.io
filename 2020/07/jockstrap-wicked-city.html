Jockstrap - Wicked City

Warp Records, 2020, glitch pop/art pop

27th July 2020


Jockstrap is a duo consisting of producer Taylor Skye and violinist Georgia Ellery, who also plays in upcoming experimental rock outfit Black Country, New Road. Wicked City is one of the weirdest projects I've heard this year but it's a good kind of weird. Opener 'Robert' is a crazy deconstructed club cut that goes from heavily processed vocals to explosive electronics and a rap verse from the late Groggs of Injury Reserve. 'Acid' is one of the best songs I've heard this year, with its soaring strings, minimal beat and surreal imagery combining for quite the crescendo. The rest of the EP is just as great, going from tranquil chamber pop to chaotic electronica in a matter of seconds. With its very art school feel, Wicked City is definitely one of my favourite projects of 2020 and I can't wait to see what these two do next.
