Clams Casino - I'm God

(No Label), 2020, cloud rap/instrumental hip hop

26th April 2020


Clams Casino has finally been able to clear the Imogen Heap sample on 'I'm God', releasing it on streaming services as part of his Instrumental Relics compilation. Originally used on Lil B's 6 Kiss, this is the beat that arguably birthed cloud rap. The instrumental is beautiful with its lush vocals forming a misty haze to lose yourself in. The echoes of "bite tongue, deep breaths" are as mesmerising and soothing as they were the first time I heard them, nearly a decade ago. This song is perfect for when you're feeling nostalgic and want to remind yourself of simpler times. A timeless classic.
