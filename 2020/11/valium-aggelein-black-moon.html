Valium Aggelein - Black Moon

Numero Group, 2020, slowcore/space rock

4th November 2020


Archival record label Numero Group continue to do God's work. Last year, they reissued Duster's entire discography as Capsule Losing Contact and this year, they've done the same for the group's pre-Duster material as Valium Aggelein. Black Moon compiles two albums recorded in 1997 and 1998 along with 4 additional tracks. Largely instrumental, it sounds similar to Stratosphere's unique blend of slowcore, space rock and lo-fi indie, but it's even more subdued and calm. Although this came out back in May, I've been listening to it a lot recently as the leaves turn orange and lockdown looms. With its mellow yet mysterious atmosphere, Black Moon serves as a great backdrop to drifting thoughts or something you can just space out to during these uncertain times.
