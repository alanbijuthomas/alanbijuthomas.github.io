The Olivia Tremor Control - Black Foliage: Animation Music Volume One

Flydaddy Records, 1999, neo-psychedelia/psychedelic pop

2nd February 2020


The Olivia Tremor Control were part of The Elephant 6 Recording Company and in my opinion, they were the best. That's right, better than Neutral Milk Hotel! Like the rest of the Elephant 6, they worshipped the psychedelic pop of the 60s, however they also had an affinity for field recordings and sound collage. On Music from the Unrealized Film Script, Dusk at Cubist Castle, these tendencies were concentrated into the 'Green Typewriters' section. However on Black Foliage, the band mix their tape experiments into their pop songs, crafting a surreal trip of a record. The way I see it, The Olivia Tremor Control made their own version of the lost SMiLE, updated to the more modern sounds of 90s indie. And even if you're not a fan of the noisy oddities like I am, there's still sunny gems like 'Hideaway' and 'A New Day'.
