Pet Shimmers - Face Down in Meta

(No Label), 2020, neo-psychedelia/indie pop

13th February 2020


Pet Shimmers are a new band from Bristol whose debut album I stumbled across on Bandcamp. With the cover's splash of colours and tags like "alternative" and "bedroom pop", it seemed like the type of record I'd enjoy. I ended up loving it and Face Down in Meta is easily the best release I've heard so far this year. Across 12 tracks running an hour long, Pet Shimmers give us a blend of sparkling melodies, dreamy guitars and kaleidoscopic electronics that recall the alt pop of Stereolab, Broadcast and even the cut-and-paste of Cornelius. While it's not the most unique or groundbreaking, Face Down in Meta has that quirky, psychedelic sound I can't seem to get enough of and tracks like 'Super Natural Teeth' and 'Mortal Sport Argonaut' have been stuck on repeat. I'm excited to see where the band goes next.
