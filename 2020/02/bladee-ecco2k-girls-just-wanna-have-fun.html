Bladee, Ecco2k - Girls just want to have fun

YEAR0001, 2020, synthpop

28th February 2020


Although I wouldn't call myself a drainer quite yet, I have been enjoying Drain Gang's music a lot more after hearing Ecco2k's E. It was one of 2019's best with its introspective themes tackled through layered vocals and otherworldly production. 'Girls just want to have fun' is a similarly beautiful song where Bladee and Ecco2k offer androgynous vocals over Whitearmor's minimal dance beat. While it sounds angelic, the duo's blissful melodies mask lyrics of despair and themes of loss. The word "ethereal" is hastily thrown onto any dreamy release these days but it really is the perfect way to describe 'Girls just want to have fun'. I can't wait to see where Drain Gang take their hybrid of cloud rap/experimental pop next.
