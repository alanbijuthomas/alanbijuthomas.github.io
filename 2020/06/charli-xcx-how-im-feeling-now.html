Charli XCX - how i'm feeling now

Asylum Records, 2020, electropop/bubblegum bass

17th June 2020


After last year's carefully crafted self-titled record, Charli XCX decided to push herself to produce another album, drawing inspiration from the effects of the coronavirus pandemic and subsequent lockdown. how i'm feeling now is intimate and direct, capturing Charli's emotions and thoughts towards the inability to socialise right now as well as detailing her long-term relationship. Sonically, it's still within the realms of bubblegum bass with its auto-tuned vocals, warped rhythms and sticky hooks. It feels a lot like Pop 2 with its experimental, free-flowing structure however the lack of features further speaks to the impossibility of collaboration right now. My favourite tracks are 'forever' with its grand synth washes and 'claws' with Dylan Brady's signature snares. It's a comforting "quarantine record" which couldn't have come at a better time and I've been dancing around to it in my bedroom for the past month. Charli hasn't missed the mark since 2016.
