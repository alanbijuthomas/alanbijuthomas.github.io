I'm Alan Biju Thomas and welcome to my blog

I'm currently a third year computer science student at the University of Manchester. Instead of just using WordPress to make a blog, I wanted to test out my web development skills so I used HTML, CSS and JavaScript along with Bootstrap and GitHub Pages to build and host this site.

I spend arguably too much time listening to music so this blog is now going to be my little space to discuss and share my thoughts on whatever I'm currently listening to, new or old. In terms of my taste, I'll listen to just about anything, but I tend to like stuff that's noisy and/or psychedelic.

Check out some other cool stuff I've done

I currently write for the Music section of The Mancunion, the University of Manchester's newspaper. Articles I've written can be found on my archive.

I hosted a radio show called 'gateway 2 a genre' on Fuse FM, the University of Manchester's radio station. Past episodes can be found on my Mixcloud.

I don't subscribe to the concept of rating art but I still use RateYourMusic to catalogue my music collection and tag stuff. Here's my RYM.
