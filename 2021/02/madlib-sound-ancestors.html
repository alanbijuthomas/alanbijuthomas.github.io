Madlib - Sound Ancestors

Madlib Invazion, 2021, instrumental hip hop

14th February 2021


After a decade highlighted by his work with Freddie Gibbs, Madlib returns in 2021 with a new solo release. Well, solo to a degree. It's not a MadTet or FourLib collaboration album as I initially expected but Four Tet is involved with the arrangement of Madlib's music. This results in a complete album experience rather than a beat tape with simple loops in the style of the Beat Konducta series. Sound Ancestors is a dense, layered tapestry of the sounds Madlib has explored over his career along with some uncharted territory. It features boom bap, jazz from around the globe and even forays into sound collage, trip hop and ambient. The gorgeous rhythms, grooves and textures make for a lush but laidback listen. Perhaps most importantly, Madlib's ear for a sample remains incredible, especially on the soulful 'Road of the Lonely Ones' and Dilla tribute 'Two for 2'. Overall, it's a fantastic album that further cements his legacy as one of hip hop's greatest icons.
