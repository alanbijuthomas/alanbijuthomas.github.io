Parannoul - To See the Next Part of the Dream

Longinus Recordings, 2021, shoegaze/emo

23rd March 2021


Parannoul is an indie musician from Seoul whose latest release has been making waves on the internet. He recommends the likes of Car Seat Headrest and Weatherday on his Bandcamp page, giving you an idea of what to expect. To See the Next Part of the Dream's lo-fi blend of shoegaze and emo works better than you'd expect. While heavy waves of crashing guitars and blasting drums dominate the album's sound, there's also calmer, more melancholic moments with twinkling melodies. The Korean vocals can serve as just texture as is standard for shoegaze releases but the lyrics are worth diving into. Parannoul tackles his depression and struggles in a nihilistic fashion, contrasting the ethereal sound of the record. 'White Ceiling' is the standout track as he pours his heart out over layers of guitars, keys and samples. To See the Next Part of the Dream is a testament to the power of online music circles and for fans of shoegaze or emo, it's definitely worth checking out.
