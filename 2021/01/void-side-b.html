Void - Side B

Dischord Records, 1982, hardcore punk

31st January 2021


Void was a short-lived band from Columbia, Maryland whose recording output was limited to Faith/Void, a split album with The Faith, another early pioneer of hardcore punk from Washington D.C. Released on the legendary Dischord Records, the album is considered a punk classic and largely due to Void's side, which now appears on streaming as Side B. For 16 minutes, Void play an unhinged, chaotic fusion of punk and metal, characterised by vicious vocals and shrieking bursts of feedback. Few hardcore bands came close to this level of intensity and they are more than deserving of their cult following. Other than Bad Brains' self-titled tape, this is the best slice of hardcore punk you'll ever hear.
